About Dataset

Title:

Simulated Dataset of Customer Purchase Behavior

Description:

This dataset contains simulated data representing customer purchase behavior. It includes various features such as age, gender, income, and education.

Data Information:

  • Number of Rows: 15
  • Number of Columns: 5

Column Descriptors:

  • ID: Unique identifier for each record (Numerical Variable)
  • Age: Age of the customer (Numerical Variable)
  • Gender: Gender of the customer (Categorical Variable: 0 for Male, 1 for Female)
  • Income: Annual income of the customer (Numerical Variable)
  • Education: Education level of the customer (Categorical Variable: Bachelor, Masters, High School, College)
